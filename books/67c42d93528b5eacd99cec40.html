Atenție cu intenție

de Dragoș Pătraru

Vezi detalii
A book about the present that challenges you to think about the future. Dragoș Pătraru, through this collection of essays inspired by the podcast 'Vocea Nației,' encourages you to take control of your most important resource: your attention. The book touches on various topics such as democracy and division, ecology and economy, and society and inequality.
