Bullshit Jobs: A Theory

de David Graeber

Vezi detalii
Bullshit Jobs: A Theory is a 2018 book by anthropologist David Graeber that discusses the existence and societal harm of meaningless jobs. It argues that over half of societal work is pointless and becomes psychologically destructive when paired with a work ethic that associates work with self-worth. The book identifies five types of meaningless jobs and discusses solutions like universal basic income.
