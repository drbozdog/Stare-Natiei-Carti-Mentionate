The Trading Game

de Gary Stevenson

Vezi detalii
The Trading Game is a vivid memoir by Gary Stevenson, a former Citibank trader, detailing his rags-to-riches story and experiences on the high-stakes trading floors of London. The book explores themes of financial excess and inequality, depicting his rise and eventual disillusionment with the trading world.
