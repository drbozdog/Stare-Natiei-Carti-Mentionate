Identitatea Virtuală

de Mihnea Măruță

Vezi detalii
The book 'Identitatea Virtuală' by Mihnea Măruță discusses how and why social networks transform us. It offers a philosophical perspective on how digital appearances affect our identities. It includes multimedia references and 10 podcast episodes featuring the author.
