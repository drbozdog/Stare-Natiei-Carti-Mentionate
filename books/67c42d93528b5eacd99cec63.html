Groundbreakers: How Obama's 2.2 Million Volunteers Transformed Campaigning in America

de Elizabeth McKenna, Hahrie Han, Jeremy Bird (Foreword)

Vezi detalii
This book details how Obama for America recruited, motivated, and managed 2.2 million volunteers. It discusses the campaign's grassroots organization and its transformative effects on the volunteers involved, making the case that it represents a new way of engaging citizens in democracy.
