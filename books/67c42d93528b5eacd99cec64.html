Carving Up the Globe: An Atlas of Diplomacy

de Malise Ruthven

Vezi detalii
Carving Up the Globe offers vivid documentation of diplomatic handiwork, illustrating treaties determining political fates over history. It features maps and images that document agreements from ancient to modern times, covering not just territorial treaties but also resources and international accords.
