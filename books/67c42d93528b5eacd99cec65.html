Bestseller international. Winner of ABIA Biography Of The Year 2021. It's a story about hope in the darkest times, featuring Eddie Jaku, a Holocaust survivor who survived Buchenwald and Auschwitz. Despite unimaginable adversity, he finds ways to rediscover happiness, share wisdom, and live a fulfilling life, thus considering himself the happiest man on Earth.
