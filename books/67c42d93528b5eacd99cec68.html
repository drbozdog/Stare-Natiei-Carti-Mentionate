Leadership à la grecque: de la Plutarh citire

de Theodor Paleologu

Vezi detalii
A book discussing the concept of leadership through the perspective of classic standards set by historical figures like the biographer Plutarch. It applies a comparative method and provides philosophical insights based on 46 case studies, ideal for those seeking inspiration from classic leadership studies.
