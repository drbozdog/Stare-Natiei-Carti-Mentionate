High Conflict: Why We Get Trapped and How We Get Out

de Amanda Ripley

Vezi detalii
High Conflict examines how individuals and groups get stuck in self-perpetuating and mutually-destructive conflicts, as well as how we can pull ourselves out of them. Through the stories of conflict survivors, Amanda Ripley reveals how to break out of destructive feuds and generate healthy, useful conflict.
