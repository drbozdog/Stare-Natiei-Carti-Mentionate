de Ana Blandiana

Vezi detalii
The book 'Mai-mult-ca-trecutul: Jurnal, 31 august 1988–12 decembrie 1989' by Ana Blandiana is a diary that captures her reflections and observations during a tumultuous time at the end of the Ceaușescu regime in Romania. It offers insights into her personal experiences and the political climate of the period, focusing on themes of loneliness, societal challenges, and the quest for freedom and meaning amidst oppression.
