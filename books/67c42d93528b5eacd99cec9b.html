de Anthony Burgess

Vezi detalii
Portocala mecanică (A Clockwork Orange) is a dystopian novel set in a near-future society where extreme youth violence prevails. The protagonist, Alex, is a sociopathic delinquent who undergoes controversial behavior modification treatment. The novel explores themes of free will, the nature of evil, and societal manipulation.
