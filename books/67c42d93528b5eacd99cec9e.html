Doctrina Social Democrata

de Florin Abraham

Vezi detalii
The document presents an analysis of the origins and stages in the evolution of European social democracy, starting with the French Revolution and ending with the Cold War. It describes the main currents of socialist and Marxist thought, as well as the international organizational structures of the democratic left, such as the First and Second Internationals.
