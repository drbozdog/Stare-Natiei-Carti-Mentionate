Inside the Mind of Marine Le Pen

de Michel Eltchaninoff

Vezi detalii
This book explores what drives Marine Le Pen and the Front National, probing the philosophical underpinnings of her political rhetoric. It addresses the transformation of the FN under her leadership while investigating the persistent influence of far-right ideologies, themes of nationalism, xenophobia, and how identity politics contribute to her political success.
