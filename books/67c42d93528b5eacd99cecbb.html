The Codul Penal refers to the Criminal Code of Romania. This legal document includes comprehensive details on criminal laws applicable in Romania, and it is used extensively by legal professionals including magistrates, lawyers, and students. The book contains the latest amendments and is designed to provide an efficient reference for legal practitioners.
