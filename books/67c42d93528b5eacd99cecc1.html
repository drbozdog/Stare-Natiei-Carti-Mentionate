Noii Precupeti: Intelectualii Publici din Romania de Dupa 1989

de Adrian Gavrilescu

Vezi detalii
The book 'Noii Precupeti' by Adrian Gavrilescu provides insights into the public intellectuals of Romania after 1989, including critical exposures on scholarly corruption and unethical behavior at various universities, such as the SNSPA. It discusses the influence and dominance of certain families and individuals in academic institutions.
