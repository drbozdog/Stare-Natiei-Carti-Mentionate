Too Big to Fail: The Inside Story of How Wall Street and Washington Fought to Save the Financial System—and Themselves

de Andrew Ross Sorkin

Vezi detalii
Too Big to Fail is a non-fiction book by Andrew Ross Sorkin that chronicles the 2008 financial crisis and the collapse of Lehman Brothers from the perspective of Wall Street CEOs and US government regulators. The book was released on October 20, 2009, and won the 2010 Gerald Loeb Award for Best Business Book. It was also adapted into an HBO movie.
