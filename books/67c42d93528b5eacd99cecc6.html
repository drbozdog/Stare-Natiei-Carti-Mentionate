Arme matematice de distrugere

de Cathy O'Neil

Vezi detalii
The book 'Arme matematice de distrugere' by Cathy O'Neil warns about the dangerous and widespread use of mathematical models without transparency, which control various life aspects, accentuating inequality and threatening democracy. Cathy O'Neil argues that these models are opaque and unregulated, fostering discrimination while masking as neutral. It is a call to scrutinize and reform these models to prevent social harm.
