Ce urmează după neoliberalism? Pentru un imaginar politic alternativ

de Sorin Gog și Victoria Stoiciu

Vezi detalii
The book discusses neoliberal transformations that have occurred in Romania over the past three decades, presenting them as an almost inevitable social and economic destiny. It critiques the minimal social state, financialization, extensive privatizations, and labor relations that have favored local and foreign investors, as well as the neoliberal consensus that has dominated Romanian political-economic history.
