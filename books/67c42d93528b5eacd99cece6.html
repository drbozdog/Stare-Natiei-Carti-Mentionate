Gustul amar al ciocolatei. Jurnalul unei supravietuitoare

de Olimpia Zamfirescu

Vezi detalii
Gustul amar al ciocolatei is partly an autobiography of Olimpia Zamfirescu, depicting her life during the communist regime in Romania. It includes detailed accounts of her time in imprisonment and forced domicile, showcasing the hardships faced during that era. The book is known for its engaging narrative style and is considered a significant work of Romanian memoir literature.
