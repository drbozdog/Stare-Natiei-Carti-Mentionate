Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind

de Yuval Noah Harari

Vezi detalii
Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind is a book by Yuval Noah Harari that explores the history and evolution of Homo sapiens, highlighting the Cognitive Revolution, Agricultural Revolution, and Scientific Revolution. The book discusses how these revolutions have shaped human societies and the world, emphasizing the role of human cooperation, ideologies, and the capacity for creating shared myths.
