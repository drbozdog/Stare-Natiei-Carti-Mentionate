The Road to Freedom: Economics and the Good Society

de Joseph E. Stiglitz

Vezi detalii
The Road to Freedom offers a powerful re-evaluation of democracy, economics and what constitutes a good society, drawing on the work of contemporary philosophers. Stiglitz critiques the neoliberal experiment and argues for a more humane and inclusive vision of freedom.
