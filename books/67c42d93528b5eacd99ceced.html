Vezi detalii
Permission to Feel: Unlocking the Power of Emotions to Help Our Kids, Ourselves, and Our Society Thrive is a bestselling book by Marc Brackett, Ph.D., the founding director of the Yale Center for Emotional Intelligence. This book presents a new mindset regarding the power of emotions to transform our lives, detailing the RULER framework as a method for developing emotional intelligence.
