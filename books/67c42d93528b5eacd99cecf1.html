The Anxious Generation

de Jonathan Haidt

Vezi detalii
The Anxious Generation: How the Great Rewiring of Childhood is Causing an Epidemic of Mental Illness explores the impact of social media and smartphones on adolescent mental health, focusing on trends starting in the early 2010s. The book offers explanations for the surge in anxiety and depression among youth and provides strategies for improving mental health.
