Drumul spre nelibertate

de Timothy Snyder

Vezi detalii
Bestseller New York Times, recommended by the editors of the New York Times, and finalist for the Lionel Gelber Prize 2019, 'Drumul spre nelibertate' by Timothy Snyder is a profound analysis of the rise of authoritarianism from Russia to Europe and America. Following the end of the Cold War, the victory of liberal democracy seemed definitive. However, authoritarianism made a comeback starting in Russia under Putin's regime, expanding from east to west, while nationalism spread across Europe. The book explores these political transformations and is regarded as a crucial examination of the current crisis of Western liberal democracy.
