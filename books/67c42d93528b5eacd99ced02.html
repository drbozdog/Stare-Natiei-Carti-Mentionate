Spre pacea eterna

de Immanuel Kant

Vezi detalii
Spre pacea eternă (Toward Perpetual Peace) is one of the most known political essays by the philosopher Immanuel Kant, published in 1795. It outlines philosophical concepts of achieving lasting peace and has influenced the formation of international structures like the European Union and the United Nations.
