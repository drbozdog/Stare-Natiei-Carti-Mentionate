Niciodată nu e de ajuns: neuroștiința și experiența dependenței

de Judith Grisel

Vezi detalii
Judith Grisel's book describes the aspects that drive drug addiction, what can help individuals recover, and argues her firm conviction that a 'treatment' for addiction cannot be found just in the brain, but in the way we interact with the community we are part of. The book combines scientific information with personal experiences, highlighting how an addict's brain differs from others, both before and after chronic use.
