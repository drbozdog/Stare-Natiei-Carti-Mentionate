Mintea Moralistă: De Ce Ne Dezbină Politica și Religia

de Jonathan Haidt

Vezi detalii
In 'Mintea Moralistă: De Ce Ne Dezbină Politica și Religia', Jonathan Haidt explores how moral psychology divides us on politics and religion but also how understanding it can promote cooperation. Haidt argues that our moral judgments are often driven more by instinct than reason, leading to polarization. The book provides a comprehensive synthesis of social psychology, political analysis, and moral reasoning.
