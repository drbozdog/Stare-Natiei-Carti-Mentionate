Hunter

de Mercedes Lackey

Vezi detalii
The book follows Joyeaux Charmand, a Hunter tasked with protecting society from dangerous creatures in a post-apocalyptic world. As Joy navigates the new societal structure in Apex City, she discovers political intrigues and uncovers conspiracies, all while reflecting on identity and societal roles.
