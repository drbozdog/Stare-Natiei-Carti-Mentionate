Why Nations Fail: The Origins of Power, Prosperity, and Poverty

de Daron Acemoglu and James A. Robinson

Vezi detalii
Why Nations Fail: The Origins of Power, Prosperity, and Poverty is a book by economists Daron Acemoglu and James A. Robinson that explores the deep-rooted causes of economic disparity among nations, emphasizing the role of political and economic institutions. The book, published in 2012, offers a wide range of historical case studies to illustrate how 'inclusive' and 'extractive' institutions impact national prosperity.
