Revenge of the Tipping Point: Overstories, Superspreaders, and the Rise of Social Engineering

de Malcolm Gladwell

Vezi detalii
Twenty-five years after the publication of his groundbreaking first book, Malcolm Gladwell returns with a brand-new volume that reframes the lessons of The Tipping Point in a startling and revealing light. The book explores how individuals can use power and influence to shape the collective narratives we tell ourselves as groups or as a society — and in so doing steward policies and perspectives.
