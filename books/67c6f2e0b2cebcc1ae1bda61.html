Peak: Secretele performantei de top si noua stiinta a expertizei

de Anders Ericsson and Robert Pool

Vezi detalii
The book demystifies exceptional achievements of extraordinary performers from musical virtuosos and distinguished scientists to neurosurgeons and professional athletes. It provides invaluable, often counterintuitive advice about setting goals, receiving feedback, identifying models, and self-motivation. Whether you want to excel in your profession, improve your athletic performance, or help your child achieve outstanding academic results, Ericsson’s revolutionary methods will show you how you can improve almost any important skill.
