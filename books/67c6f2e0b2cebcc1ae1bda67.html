Vieti Paralele

de Plutarh

Vezi detalii
"Vieti Paralele" by Plutarh is a series consisting of 50 biographies of notable Greek and Roman individuals, including statesmen, soldiers, and orators. 46 of them are written in parallel (one Greek and one Roman), while 4 present unique personalities. These biographies focus on the characters and destinies of these individuals rather than historical events, offering ethical insights and moral examples. The work is recognized for its historical value and Plutarch's approach to exploring virtues and failings through individual lives.
