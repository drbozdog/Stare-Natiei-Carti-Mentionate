Marea Renastere. Adevar, libertate, suveranitate. Ieșirea din Matrix

de Călin Georgescu

Vezi detalii
The book presents a vision for a self-sufficient society, critiquing material wealth as an end in itself and proposing a system based on simplicity. Georgescu emphasizes personal sovereignty and independence from global systems, aiming to form self-sufficient communities.
