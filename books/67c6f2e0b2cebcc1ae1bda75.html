Vezi detalii
1984, written by George Orwell, is a dystopian novel published in 1949 as a cautionary tale about the perils of totalitarianism. It is set in a society under the omnipresent surveillance of the Party and its leader, Big Brother, and it explores themes of censorship, identity, and the impact of oppressive government. The protagonist, Winston Smith, seeks truth and freedom in a world where individual thought is criminalized.
