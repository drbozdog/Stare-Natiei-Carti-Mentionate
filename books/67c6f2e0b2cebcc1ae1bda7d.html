Patul lui Procust

de Camil Petrescu

Vezi detalii
Patul lui Procust is a psychological novel written by Camil Petrescu, first published in February 1933, in two volumes, by Editura Națională Ciornei. The novel centers on the limitations of human beings in the universe and in consciousness, manifested through the obsession with measuring and being measured by the world and oneself.
