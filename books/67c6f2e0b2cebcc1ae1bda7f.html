The Narrow Corridor

de Daron Acemoglu and James A. Robinson

Vezi detalii
In their book, 'The Narrow Corridor,' Acemoglu and Robinson explore the complexities of democracy, discussing how liberty is achieved through a delicate balance between state and society. They provide insights with historical and current examples about how societies can secure or lose freedom.
