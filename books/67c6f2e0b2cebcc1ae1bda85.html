The High Cost of Free Parking

de Donald Shoup

Vezi detalii
The High Cost of Free Parking is a seminal work in urban planning by Donald Shoup, who provides a comprehensive analysis of parking policies and their impact on urban development. The book argues that free parking has detrimental effects on cities, proposing reforms to make better use of parking spaces.
