Un cal intră într-un bar

de David Grossman

Vezi detalii
Un cal intră într-un bar is a novel by David Grossman that won the Man Booker International Prize in 2017. The story unfolds in the Israeli town of Netanya and revolves around a stand-up comedy performance by the protagonist, Dovaleh Greenstein. What starts as a usual comedic routine slowly delves into a dramatic introspection, reflecting on a traumatic event from his past. Grossman uses the setting to explore themes like grief, regret, and the human condition, through a narrative filled with both humor and poignancy.
