Istoria Poloniei. Terenul de Joacă al lui Dumnezeu

de Norman Davies

Vezi detalii
The book 'Istoria Poloniei. Terenul de Joacă al lui Dumnezeu' by Norman Davies provides a detailed history of Poland. It covers Poland's history from its origins up to the present day, highlighting its role in European history, cultural heritage, and constitutional aspects. It is a two-volume series covering the periods from origins to 1795 and from 1795 to the present.
