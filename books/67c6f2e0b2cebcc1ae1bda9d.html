Țigan țăndări

de Valeriu Nicolae

Vezi detalii
Țigan țăndări is an autobiographical docu-fiction book by journalist and human rights activist Valeriu Nicolae. It explores his childhood and the challenging realities of adult life. The book includes some raw and complex stories that he had never dared to share before. Valeriu Nicolae's revenue from this book has been used to support disadvantaged children in Ferentari, helping them stay in school.
