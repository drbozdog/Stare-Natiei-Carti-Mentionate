de Robert M. Sapolsky

Vezi detalii
The book 'Determinat: O știință a vieții fără liber arbitru' by Robert M. Sapolsky discusses the lack of free will and how decisions are predetermined by a complex range of factors beyond our control. Sapolsky challenges the traditional notions of free will using insights from neurobiology, philosophy, quantum physics, and other sciences, advocating for a more empathetic view of human behavior.
