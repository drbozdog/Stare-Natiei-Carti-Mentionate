Carte de bucate

de Sanda Marin

Vezi detalii
This is a comprehensive Romanian cookbook by Sanda Marin that has been a staple in Romanian kitchens since its first publication in 1936. It contains over 9,000 recipes and has inspired countless other cookbooks. The book has undergone several editions and is a beloved culinary guide for both new and experienced cooks.
