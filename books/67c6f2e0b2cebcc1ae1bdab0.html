Vezi detalii
Rezerva is the Romanian edition of Prince Harry's memoir, Spare. It is a candid account of his life, his struggles with the royalty, mental health, and personal experiences. The memoir provides insight into his life beyond the public image, addressing intimate aspects and controversial topics.
