Când creierul lucrează împotriva ta

de Michael Easter

Vezi detalii
The book explores why we repeat harmful behaviors and offers insights into how we can stop them, even when our brain seems to be working against us. It provides scientific perspectives, personal stories, and practical guidance to help readers understand the deep roots of their urges and how to deal with them to lead a more meaningful life.
