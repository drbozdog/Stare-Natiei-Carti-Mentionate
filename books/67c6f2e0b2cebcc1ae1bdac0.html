Căldură Mare

de Ion Luca Caragiale

Vezi detalii
Căldură mare is a sketch by Ion Luca Caragiale that depicts a humorous situation during a hot summer day at 33°C. It involves characters displaying an imperturbable calmness amid confusion about the whereabouts of a person they are trying to contact.
