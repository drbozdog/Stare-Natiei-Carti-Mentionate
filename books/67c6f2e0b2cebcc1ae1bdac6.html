How to Make the World Add Up

de Tim Harford

Vezi detalii
In 'How to Make the World Add Up', Tim Harford presents ten lessons that help us understand the often overwhelming world of data and statistics. Through engaging storytelling, he explains how to see beyond our biases and emotional reactions, urging readers to maintain curiosity and a healthy skepticism while avoiding gullibility. Harford discusses historical figures like Florence Nightingale and explores various intriguing anecdotes to make statistics accessible and relevant.
