Bani: O poveste a omenirii

de David McWilliams

Vezi detalii
Bani: O poveste a omenirii by David McWilliams is a compelling exploration of the history and influence of money on human civilization. McWilliams presents money as a crucial social technology that has driven innovation, societal changes, and the evolution of civilizations across history. The book travels through various historical epochs, examining the role of money from ancient times to modern day, providing insights into how money has functioned as a necessity and a powerful influencer in human development.
