The Last Illegal Courier - The Memoirs of a Romanian Spy

de Cornel Nemetzi (Nemeti)

Vezi detalii
The memoirs include experiences of a former Romanian spy officer detailing espionage activities, particularly focusing on the division known as 'The Ghosts'. It sheds light on recruitment and operations from within Romanian intelligence services like the DIE and SIE.
