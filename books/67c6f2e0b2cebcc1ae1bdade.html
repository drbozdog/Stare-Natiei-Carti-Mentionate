Four Thousand Weeks: Time Management for Mortals

de Oliver Burkeman

Vezi detalii
Four Thousand Weeks: Time Management for Mortals is a 2021 non-fiction book written by British author Oliver Burkeman. The title draws from the premise that 'the average human lifespan is absurdly, terrifyingly, insultingly short... Assuming you live to be eighty, you’ll have had about four thousand weeks.' It explores the modern relationship with time and how to make the most of a finite existence, arguing for embracing limits, accepting uncertainty, and focusing on what truly matters.
