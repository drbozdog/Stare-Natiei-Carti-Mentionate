23 de lucruri care nu ți se spun despre capitalism

de Ha-Joon Chang

Vezi detalii
The book is a critical examination of capitalism, addressing various misconceptions including the myth of free markets and the alleged benefits of globalization. It presents evidence showing that poorer countries often exhibit greater entrepreneurial spirit than wealthier nations, and discusses how higher compensation for management does not always correlate with better performance.
