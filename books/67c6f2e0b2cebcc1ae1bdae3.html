Playing to Win

de Hilary Levey Friedman

Vezi detalii
Playing to Win: Raising Children in a Competitive Culture follows the path of elementary school-age children involved in competitive dance, youth travel soccer, and scholastic chess. The book contextualizes children's activities, suggesting they serve as proving grounds for success, especially towards elite university admissions. It provides insight into how competitive environments shape the lives and perspectives of children and their parents in America.
