Utopia for Realists

de Rutger Bregman

Vezi detalii
Utopia for Realists: The Case for a Universal Basic Income, Open Borders, and a 15-hour Workweek is a book by Dutch popular historian Rutger Bregman. It challenges the traditional notions of society and economics, presenting bold ideas such as universal basic income and open borders as solutions to modern problems.
