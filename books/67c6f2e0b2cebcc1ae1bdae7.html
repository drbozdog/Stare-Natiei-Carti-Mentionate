Ministerul pentru viitor

de Kim Stanley Robinson

Vezi detalii
The Ministry for the Future is a climate fiction novel that explores the establishment of a global organization, the Ministry for the Future, created under the Paris Agreement to advocate for future generations affected by climate change. The narrative follows characters including Mary Murphy, the head of the Ministry, as they tackle the existential threats posed by climate change through various projects and initiatives aimed at sustainable solutions.
