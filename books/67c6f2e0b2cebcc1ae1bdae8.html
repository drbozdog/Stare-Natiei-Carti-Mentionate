My Brilliant Friend

de Elena Ferrante

Vezi detalii
The series includes four novels: My Brilliant Friend, The Story of a New Name, Those Who Leave and Those Who Stay, and The Story of the Lost Child. It follows the lifelong friendship of Elena and Lila, two girls from a poor neighborhood in Naples, exploring themes of identity, personal growth, and the complexities of friendship over the years.
