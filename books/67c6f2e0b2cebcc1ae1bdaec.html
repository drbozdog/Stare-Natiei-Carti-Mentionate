De ce muncim?

de Universitatea Babeș-Bolyai

Vezi detalii
The study 'De ce muncim?' conducted by the Center for Strategy, Leadership, and Intelligent Development at Babeș-Bolyai University analyzes the work-life balance and professional identity among young employees and students in Cluj-Napoca. It highlights a shift in work behavior, focusing on the importance of balancing professional and personal life, and aiming to redefine the professional identity, influencing satisfaction and commitment towards work.
