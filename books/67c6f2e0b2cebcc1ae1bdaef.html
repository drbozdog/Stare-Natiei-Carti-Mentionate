But What Can I Do?

de Alastair Campbell

Vezi detalii
This book explores the need for political engagement and highlights how people can take action within their communities and beyond. Alastair Campbell combines his political experience and insights to empower individuals to understand that, though they may feel powerless, they can effect change through advocacy and public engagement.
