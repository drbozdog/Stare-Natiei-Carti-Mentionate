The Bastard of Istanbul

de Elif Shafak

Vezi detalii
Populated by strong and memorable female figures, 'The Bastard of Istanbul' is a fascinating novel intertwining freedom and oppression, reality and fantasy, memory and forgetfulness. The Eastern world, with its exoticism and traditionalism, meets unexpectedly with the Western world, marked by pragmatism and independence, through the unrestrained desires of two charming and nonconformist young women, Asya and Armanoush, to uncover the mystery surrounding their family roots and discover their own identities.
