Darul

de Edit Eva Eger

Vezi detalii
In 'Darul', Edith Eva Eger shares her transformative journey of healing and empowerment after surviving unimaginable horrors. She describes 12 significant emotional challenges such as fear, pain, rage, guilt, and shame, as well as the tools she discovered to face these universal challenges. The book emphasizes that what matters most is not what happens to us, but how we choose to respond to those events.
