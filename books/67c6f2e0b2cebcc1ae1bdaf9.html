The Improbable President

de Vincent O'Leary

Vezi detalii
This book discusses the circumstances surrounding Donald Trump's unexpected rise to the presidency, exploring the socio-political climate that allowed for such an improbable candidacy. It reflects on broader themes of dissatisfaction with the ruling class and critique of the structures of democracy.
