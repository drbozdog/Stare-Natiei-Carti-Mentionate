The 3 'macronutrients of happiness'

de Arthur C. Brooks

Vezi detalii
Arthur C. Brooks discusses three key components essential for a fulfilling life: enjoyment, satisfaction, and purpose, akin to macronutrients in a diet. The book emphasizes that happiness is not a destination but a continuous journey that requires a balance of these elements.
