Plecat

de Elena Stancu și Cosmin Bumbuț

Vezi detalii
The book is an extension of a longform project titled 'Away,' which documents the lives of Romanian migrants across Europe, focusing on their challenges, the reasons they emigrated, and the impact on their families back in Romania. Through personal stories and extensive fieldwork, the authors aim to uncover the realities faced by these individuals and contribute nuanced perspectives to the portrayal of the Romanian diaspora.
