Istoria filozofiei occidentale, Volumul 2

Vezi detalii
This book presents a detailed account of the history of Western philosophy from ancient Greek philosophy to modern times, addressing various philosophical ideas and movements. It aims to be accessible to the general public, while also providing in-depth insights into the thoughts of notable philosophers.
