Ignorance is Bliss

de Stephen Mitchell

Vezi detalii
A political satire wherein one very powerful Senator is able to turn the entire country upside-down so as to match the conditions of his own personal life. Stephen Mitchell’s Ignorance is Bliss takes us into the myopic and inebriated world of Martin Bliss, a senior Senator and an important figure on the Senate Intelligence Committee. He comes from a privileged background—his father held sway over the Senate Ways and Means Committee—and Martin is striving to create his own legacy.
