The Social Dilemma

de Jeff Orlowski, Davis Coombe, and Vickie Curtis

Vezi detalii
The Social Dilemma is a 2020 American docudrama film that explores the detrimental social effects of social media. The documentary features interviews with tech experts and a dramatized narrative that illustrates the impact of social media on family dynamics and individual behavior. It discusses the manipulation techniques used by social media platforms to addict users and explores issues such as mental health, fake news, and the implications for democracy.
