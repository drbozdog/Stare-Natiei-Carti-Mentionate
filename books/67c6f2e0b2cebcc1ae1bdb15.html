Luceafărul

de Mihai Eminescu

Vezi detalii
Luceafărul is a narrative poem by Romanian author Mihai Eminescu, first published in 1883. It is considered Eminescu's masterpiece and one of the greatest works of Romanian literature. The poem tells the story of the Morning Star (Hyperion) who falls in love with a mortal princess, Cătălina, exploring themes of love, immortality, and the unattainable nature of human desires. It combines elements of folklore, Romantic themes, and Indo-European mythology, embodying a philosophical meditation on the nature of genius and love.
