Teatrul națiunii

de Anca Hatiegan

Vezi detalii
This book is part of the Encyclopedia of Imaginaries from Romania, focusing on the themes of national identity and theatre within the Romanian context. It discusses various aspects of how theatrical representations are intertwined with notions of nationhood, cultural memory, and national identity.
