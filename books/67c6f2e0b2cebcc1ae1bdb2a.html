Robin and the Backstabbers is a Romanian band that released three albums in their trilogies labeled Bacovia Overdrive, capturing a blend of various music styles while reflecting on nuanced themes about life, ambitions, and experiences. The first album, 'Bacovia Overdrive vol. 1: Stalingrad', was well received critically and was released as a part of the trilogy, followed by 'Bacovia Overdrive vol. 2: Arhanghel'sk' and 'Bacovia Overdrive vol. 3: Vladivostok'.
