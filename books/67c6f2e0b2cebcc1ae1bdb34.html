Sooley

de John Grisham

Vezi detalii
In his first basketball novel, John Grisham takes you on a journey with Samuel 'Sooley' Sooleymon, a talented young athlete from South Sudan facing devastating challenges. After receiving an opportunity to showcase his skills in the United States, he learns of the civil war ravaging his homeland. Determined to succeed and bring his family to safety, Sooley dedicates himself to the game, showcasing Grisham's unmatched storytelling ability in an emotional and gripping narrative.
