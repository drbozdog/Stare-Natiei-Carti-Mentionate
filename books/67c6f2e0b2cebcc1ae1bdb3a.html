Eu nu strivesc corola de minuni a lumii

de Lucian Blaga

Vezi detalii
The poem 'Eu nu strivesc corola de minuni a lumii' explores the relationship between knowledge and mystery, emphasizing the importance of respecting and amplifying the beauty and complexity of the world. Lucian Blaga portrays the role of the poet as one who enhances the mystery of existence rather than diminishing it through rational analysis.
