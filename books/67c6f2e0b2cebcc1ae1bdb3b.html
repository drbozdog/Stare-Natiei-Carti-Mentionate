How We Learn

de Stanislas Dehaene

Vezi detalii
An illuminating dive into the latest science on our brain’s remarkable learning abilities and the potential of the machines we program to imitate them. The human brain is an extraordinary learning machine. Its ability to reprogram itself is unparalleled, and it remains the best source of inspiration for recent developments in artificial intelligence. In 'How We Learn', Stanislas Dehaene explains how learning really works and how to make the best use of the brain’s learning algorithms in our schools and universities, as well as in everyday life and at any age.
