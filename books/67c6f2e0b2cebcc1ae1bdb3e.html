Fabrica de doctorate sau Cum se surpă fundamentele unei nații

de Emilia Șercan

Vezi detalii
This book discusses the proliferation of the phenomenon of plagiarism in Romania's doctoral theses and critiques the educational system's failures post-1989. It delves into various cases of academic dishonesty and the broader implications on national integrity.
