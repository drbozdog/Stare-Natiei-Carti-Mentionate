The Haret

de Denise (Daisy) Parton

Vezi detalii
Londyn Rebold has a haunting secret, but before she can tell her best friend, she mysteriously disappears. Unnerved by her disappearance, her small town unites to search for their missing teen. During the investigation, a peculiar Nocturnal Journal is discovered, hidden in her bedroom. The journal reveals cryptic messages along with detailed encounters with an enticing nighttime visitor. A week later, Londyn is found roaming the woods, confused and unaware of where she has spent the past seven days. With the help of her estranged grandmother, Londyn finds the answers to the secrets surrounding her missing week and traces her roots back ten generations, making a startling discovery that someone very close to her is not who they seem.
