Mănăstirea Argeșului

de Traditional ballad collected by Vasile Alecsandri

Vezi detalii
Mănăstirea Argeșului is a renowned Romanian ballad that recounts the legend of the construction of a monastery during the reign of Negru Vodă. The master builder Manole is tasked with building the monastery. However, the walls collapse each night until a human sacrifice, his wife Ana, is made. The tale reflects themes of sacrifice, dedication, and the intertwining of fate and love.
