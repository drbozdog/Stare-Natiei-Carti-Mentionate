Interviul cu profesorul Mircea Miclea

de Mircea Miclea

Vezi detalii
The book discusses the educational reforms in Romania, the shortcomings of the education system, and the necessity of a more effective and appealing educational model. Mircea Miclea, as a prominent psychologist and politician, shares insights gathered from his extensive experience in education, emphasizing the importance of understanding the role of parents, teachers, and the systemic issues that hinder educational progress.
