Trickle Down Theory and Tax Cuts for the Rich

de Thomas Sowell

Vezi detalii
This essay unscrambles gross misconceptions that have made rational debates about tax policies virtually impossible for decades. It critiques the arguments surrounding trickle-down economics and presents evidence against the idea that benefits for the wealthy automatically translate to broader prosperity.
