How to Do Nothing: Resisting the Attention Economy

de Jenny Odell

Vezi detalii
How to Do Nothing is a dense but introspective book that makes the case for redefining productivity outside neoliberal pressures and emphasizes the importance of regaining control over our attention. It discusses the concepts of mutual responsibility and conscious engagement with the world around us.
