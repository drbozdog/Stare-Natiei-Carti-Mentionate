Are You Dave Gorman?

de Dave Gorman and Danny Wallace

Vezi detalii
Are You Dave Gorman? is a stage show and book co-written by comedian Dave Gorman and writer Danny Wallace. The work originated from a drunken bet where Gorman claimed that there were many individuals named Dave Gorman, leading him and Wallace on a journey to meet various Dave Gormans. It features a mix of comedy and documentary style, discussing identity and coincidence.
