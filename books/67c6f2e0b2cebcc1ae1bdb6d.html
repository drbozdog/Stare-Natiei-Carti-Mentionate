Psychopolitics: Neoliberalism and New Technologies of Power

de Byung-Chul Han

Vezi detalii
Exploring how neoliberalism has discovered the productive force of the psyche, Byung-Chul Han continues his critique of neoliberalism, describing a regime of technological domination. The book discusses neoliberal psychopolitics and proposes counter models, presenting ideas and alternatives.
