Limitarianism: The Case Against Extreme Wealth

de Ingrid Robeyns

Vezi detalii
An original and galvanizing indictment of the world’s uber-rich that boldly argues for a cap on wealth from the philosopher who coined the term 'limitarianism.' How much money is too much? Economist and philosopher Ingrid Robeyns has long written and argued for the principle she calls 'limitarianism' – a 'common sense' case against extreme wealth, which posits that a considered cap on one's individual wealth is an urgent ethical concern that will ultimately lead to healthier and more democratic societies.
