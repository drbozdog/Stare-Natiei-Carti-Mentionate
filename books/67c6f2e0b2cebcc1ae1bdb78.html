Ce este populismul?

de Jan-Werner Müller

Vezi detalii
Jan-Werner Müller's 'Ce este populismul?' explores the concept of populism by examining case studies from Europe, the United States, and Latin America. The book discusses how populism pits the 'pure' people against a corrupt elite, critiques the threats it poses to liberal democracy, and offers strategies for confronting it.
