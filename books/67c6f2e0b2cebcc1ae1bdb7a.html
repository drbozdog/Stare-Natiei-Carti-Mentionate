Epoca furiei: O istorie a prezentului

de Pankaj Mishra

Vezi detalii
The book explores the contradictions and turmoil that accompanied the rise of industrial capitalism in 19th century Europe, and how these conflicts have spread globally. It addresses contemporary issues of populism, nationalism, and extremism, placing them in a historical context. The book suggests that many of today's political, social, and economic challenges are rooted in these longstanding tensions.
