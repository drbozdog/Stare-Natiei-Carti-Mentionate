Politics

de Aristotle

Vezi detalii
Politics (Πολιτικά, Politiká) is a work of political philosophy by Aristotle, a 4th-century BC Greek philosopher. It explores topics like the relationship between ethics and politics, the nature of communities, and Aristotle's views on citizenship and governance. His comprehensive study outlines different forms of government, the role of education, and the structure of a just society.
