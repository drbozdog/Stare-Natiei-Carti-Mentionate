Mai-mult-ca-trecutul. Jurnal, 31 august 1988-12 decembrie 1989

de Ana Blandiana

Vezi detalii
The journal covers a critical period from August 31, 1988, to December 12, 1989, during which Ana Blandiana reflects on the societal and personal struggles under the communist regime in Romania. The narrative emphasizes themes of loneliness, distrust, and the human spirit's resilience amidst adversity.
