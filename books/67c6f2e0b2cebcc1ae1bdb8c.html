Jurnalul fericirii

de Nicolae Steinhardt

Vezi detalii
Jurnalul fericirii is a profound philosophical and theological work, blending memories, reflections, and essays by Nicolae Steinhardt, a Romanian writer and Orthodox monk. The book, which reflects on his experiences, including his time in prison, offers insights into faith, freedom, happiness, and human resilience.
