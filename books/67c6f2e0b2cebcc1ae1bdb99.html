Doughnut Economics: Seven Ways to Think Like a 21st-Century Economist

de Kate Raworth

Vezi detalii
Doughnut Economics offers a fresh, holistic perspective on economic theory. It critiques outdated economic models and proposes a new framework, encapsulated by the 'doughnut' concept, which balances essential human needs and planetary sustainability. The book integrates insights from diverse fields like ecological, behavioral, and feminist economics, aiming to guide development that serves people and the planet.
