Jurnal 1935-1944

de Mihail Sebastian

Vezi detalii
Mihail Sebastian's diary, kept during the years 1935 to 1944, offers a significant chronicle of the rise of antisemitism in Romania and Europe. It highlights the author's personal experiences amid the growing fascist climate before and during World War II.
