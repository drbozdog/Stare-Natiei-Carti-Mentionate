Ultimul Curier Ilegal. Memoriile unui spion roman

de Cornel Nemetzi

Vezi detalii
"Ultimul Curier Ilegal. Memoriile unui spion roman" is a factual book presenting over 90% real events experienced by Cornel Nemetzi. It reveals lesser-known aspects of Romanian espionage history, shedding light on both dedicated patriots and traitors involved in this secretive service during and after the communist era.
