1979

de Christian Kracht

Vezi detalii
1979 is a 2001 novel by the Swiss writer Christian Kracht, set in the year 1979. The story follows a homosexual young man and his ex-boyfriend in Tehran during the Iranian Revolution, and later events lead him to Tibet where he is captured by the Chinese army. It explores themes of political turmoil and personal detachment.
