Citizens

de Jon Alexander and Arian Conrad

Vezi detalii
Citizens opens up a new way of understanding ourselves and shows us what we must do to survive and thrive as individuals, organisations, and nations. The authors argue for a shift away from seeing people as consumers to recognizing them as empowered citizens. The book includes insights and case studies from various global contexts, aimed at inspiring active participation in democracy and community engagement.
