The Element: How Finding Your Passion Changes Everything

de Sir Ken Robinson

Vezi detalii
In 'The Element', Sir Ken Robinson explores the intersection of natural talent and personal passion, arguing that finding one's 'Element' is essential for true achievement and fulfillment in life. The book includes inspiring stories of successful individuals who have found their Element, and offers insights on how to recognize and nurture these passions.
