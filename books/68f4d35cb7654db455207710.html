Peak: Secrets from the New Science of Expertise

de K. Anders Ericsson, Robert Pool

Vezi detalii
Peak: Secrets from the New Science of Expertise is a 2016 book that synthesizes decades of research into the nature and acquisition of expertise, emphasizing deliberate practice as the key to skill development and superior performance. It challenges the misconception of talent being inherent and outlines methods for acquiring and refining skills through focused and purposeful practice.
