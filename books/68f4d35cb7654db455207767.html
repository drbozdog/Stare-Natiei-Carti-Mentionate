Economia: The Broad Field of Economics Revealed

de Kapil S. Joshi

Vezi detalii
Economia offers a comprehensive exploration of economics, blending foundational principles with innovative and groundbreaking theories. The book covers essential topics like Microeconomics and Macroeconomics, providing insights into the functioning of individuals, markets, and governments. It delves into Behavioral Economics, uncovering the influence of human psychology on decision-making, and Environmental Economics, addressing sustainability challenges. The journey extends to Political Economics, examining how power and policy shape economic outcomes, and ventures into less familiar realms such as Chaos Theory, Institutional Economics, Revolutionary Economics, and the Paradox of Thrift. A unique section on Religion and Economics explores the impact of faith on economic behavior across diverse societies. Finally, Economia investigates the growth and evolution of India's economy, analyzing current strategies and future opportunities. Whether you're a student or a curious reader, this book offers a clear and accessible understanding of the forces driving the global and Indian economies.
