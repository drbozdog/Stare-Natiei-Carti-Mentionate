Eseuri

de Umberto Eco

Vezi detalii
Eseuri (Essays) by Umberto Eco is a collection of essays that explore various subjects, often reflecting on culture, literature, and society. In these essays, Eco discusses the moral vigilance necessary to combat extremism and discusses education's role in society, addressing themes related to functional illiteracy and the dangers of political extremism.
