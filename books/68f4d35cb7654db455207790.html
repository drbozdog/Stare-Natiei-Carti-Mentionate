Our Lives in Their Portfolios

de Brett Christophers

Vezi detalii
As the owners of more and more of the basic building blocks of everyday life, asset managers shape the lives of each and every one of us in profound and disturbing ways. In this eye-opening follow-up to Rentier Capitalism, Brett Christophers examines how asset managers have acquired not only financial assets but also critical infrastructure, influencing significant aspects of our lives.
