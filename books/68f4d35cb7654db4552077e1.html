Power and Progress

de Daron Acemoglu and Simon Johnson

Vezi detalii
Awarded the 2024 Nobel Prize in Economics, Daron Acemoglu and Simon Johnson overturn conventional wisdom about how economies work–revealing the untold story of who wins and who loses the rewards of prosperity–in a work that fundamentally transforms how we look at and understand the world. They argue that technology is shaped by powerful interests and not necessarily linked to public prosperity, making the case for policies that ensure shared benefits.
