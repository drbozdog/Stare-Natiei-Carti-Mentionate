Baltagul

de Mihail Sadoveanu

Vezi detalii
The Hatchet (orig. Romanian: Baltagul) is a 1930 crime novel that tells the story of Vitoria Lipan, a shepherd's wife who embarks on a journey to find her missing husband, Nechifor. The novel explores themes of premonition, justice, and the role of women in traditional Romanian society. It is regarded as one of Mihail Sadoveanu's greatest works and creatively adapts themes from the famous Romanian ballad Miorița.
