Patriot: A Memoir

de Alexei Navalny

Vezi detalii
Patriot: A Memoir is a compelling and powerful memoir by Russian opposition leader Alexei Navalny. It details his life, his political activism, his imprisonment, and his relentless fight for freedom in Russia. The book is infused with Navalny's humor, idealism, and a deep commitment to the values of individual freedom. Patriot is not only a personal narrative but also a historic text documenting the challenges faced under an autocratic regime and serves as a testament to resilience, courage, and the ongoing struggle for democracy.
