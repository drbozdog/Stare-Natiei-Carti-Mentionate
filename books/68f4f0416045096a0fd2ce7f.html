The Road to Serfdom

de Friedrich Hayek

Vezi detalii
The Road to Serfdom is a book by Austrian-British economist and philosopher Friedrich Hayek, warning of the danger of tyranny that inevitably results from government control of economic decision-making through central planning. It argues that abandonment of individualism and classical liberalism inevitably leads to oppressive societies.
