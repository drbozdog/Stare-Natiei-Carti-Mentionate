Free Will

de Sam Harris

Vezi detalii
Free Will by Sam Harris is a 2012 book that argues free will is an illusion, addressing how this perspective influences morality and society's conceptions about human behavior. Harris explores the philosophical and scientific dimensions, proposing that all human actions arise from preceding causes.
