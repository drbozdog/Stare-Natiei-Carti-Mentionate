Amintiri din copilărie

de Ion Creangă

Vezi detalii
‘Amintiri din copilărie’ is a memoir by Ion Creangă that provides a detailed account of his childhood in the region of Moldova, then part of Romania. The narrative is famous for its first-person portrayal of rural life and societal conditions during Creangă's early years, filled with humorous anecdotes and profound insights into his traditions and family relationships. The book is considered a classic in Romanian literature, often praised for its linguistic style and depiction of Moldavian dialect.
