Vezi detalii
The Book of Kells is an illuminated manuscript Gospel book in Latin, containing the four Gospels of the New Testament along with various prefatory texts and tables. It was created by Celtic monks around 800 AD in a Columban monastery in either Ireland or Scotland. The manuscript is famous for its lavish decoration, combining Christian iconography with the swirling motifs typical of Insular art.
