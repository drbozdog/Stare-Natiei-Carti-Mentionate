The Art of Loving

de Erich Fromm

Vezi detalii
The Art of Loving is a 1956 book by psychoanalyst and social philosopher Erich Fromm. In this work, Fromm critiques the popular conception of love and asserts that love is an art that requires practice and dedication. He discusses various forms of love and emphasizes the importance of self-love and the ability to love others as essential components of mature love.
