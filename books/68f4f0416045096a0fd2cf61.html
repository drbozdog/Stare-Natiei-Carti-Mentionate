Peak: Secrets from the New Science of Expertise

de Anders Ericsson and Robert Pool

Vezi detalii
In 'Peak', Anders Ericsson and Robert Pool argue that the widely held belief that it takes 10,000 hours of practice to achieve expertise is oversimplified. They emphasize the importance of deliberate practice—practice that is purposeful, systematic, and focused on improving performance—not just the amount of practice time. The book provides insights into how this type of practice can lead to extraordinary performance and how anyone can apply these principles to their own learning and development.
