Poveștile Copilăriei Vol 1 is an interactive storybook designed for children that includes classic tales such as Scufița Roșie, Cenușăreasa, Frumoasa din Pădurea Adormită, and Hansel și Gretel. It features sound elements to enhance the reading experience, making it a multisensory adventure.
