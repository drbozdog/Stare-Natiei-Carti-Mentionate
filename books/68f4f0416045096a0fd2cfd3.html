Capra cu trei iezi

de Ion Creangă

Vezi detalii
Capra cu trei iezi ('The Goat and Her Three Kids') is an 1875 short story, fable, and fairy tale by Romanian author Ion Creangă. The story illustrates themes of motherly love, childish disobedience, and revenge, recounting the tale of a goat whose two ill-behaved kids are eaten by a wolf, while the obedient youngest kid survives, helping his mother to ultimately take revenge on the predator.
