The Eye of the Master: A Social History of Artificial Intelligence

de Matteo Pasquinelli

Vezi detalii
A social history of AI that finally reveals its roots in the spatial computation of industrial factories and the surveillance of collective behavior. The book challenges the myths about artificial intelligence (AI) being a quest to imitate biological intelligence, arguing instead it's a continuation of the automation of labor.
