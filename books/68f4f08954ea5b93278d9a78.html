Moscova provoacă lumea

de Ion Rațiu

Vezi detalii
The book 'Moscova provoacă lumea' is a visionary work by Ion Rațiu, originally written in 1949-1950 and later published in London in 1986. It discusses the imperial ambitions of Russia and serves as a prophetic testament about the dangers of Soviet expansionism. It draws parallels with the intellectual climate of Orwell’s 1984 and critiques the communist threats during the Cold War.
