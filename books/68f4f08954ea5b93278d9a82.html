Digital Minimalism: Choosing a Focused Life in a Noisy World

de Cal Newport

Vezi detalii
In this timely and enlightening book, bestselling author Cal Newport introduces a philosophy for technology use that has already improved countless lives. It applies the idea of digital minimalism to our personal technology and presents a method for deciding what tools to use, for what purposes, and under what conditions.
