România Educată. Raport al Președintelui României, Klaus Iohannis

de Klaus Iohannis

Vezi detalii
România Educată is a comprehensive report released by Klaus Iohannis, President of Romania, detailing a strategic vision for educational reform in Romania through 2030. The report outlines several challenges and proposes solutions to improve the educational landscape, focusing on personalized education, flexibility, and adaptability to societal changes.
