Il fascismo eterno

de Umberto Eco

Vezi detalii
Il fascismo eterno is a collection of essays by Umberto Eco exploring the philosophical and historical aspects of fascism. The key essay, 'Ur-Fascism,' presents 14 characteristics of what Eco terms 'eternal fascism,' highlighting how these traits can manifest in various political forms today.
