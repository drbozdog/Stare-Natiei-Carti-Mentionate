Moartea lui Ivan Ilici

de Leo Tolstoy

Vezi detalii
Moartea lui Ivan Ilici (Russian: Смерть Ивана Ильича) is a novella by Leo Tolstoy first published in 1886. It tells the story of Ivan Ilyich, a high-ranking judge in 19th century Russia, who faces a terminal illness that forces him to confront the meaning of his life and death. The novella explores deep themes of mortality, the authenticity of life, and the societal norms surrounding death. Tolstoy illuminates the protagonist’s journey from a superficial existence to a profound realization of what it truly means to live.
