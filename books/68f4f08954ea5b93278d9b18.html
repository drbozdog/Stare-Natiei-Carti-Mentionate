Noise: A Flaw in Human Judgment

de Daniel Kahneman, Olivier Sibony, Cass Sunstein

Vezi detalii
Noise: A Flaw in Human Judgment explores the concept of 'noise' in human judgment and decision-making, defined as undesirable variability in judgments of the same problem. The authors provide various examples of noise in different fields, such as medicine and criminal law, and discuss its implications for fairness and accuracy in decisions.
