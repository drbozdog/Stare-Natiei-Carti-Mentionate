There Is No Planet B: A Handbook for the Make or Break Years

de Mike Berners-Lee

Vezi detalii
There Is No Planet B maps it out in an accessible and entertaining way, filled with astonishing facts and analysis about how humanity can thrive in the twenty-first century. The book provides realistic hope while addressing pressing global issues like climate change, biodiversity, and sustainable living. It details practical steps individuals and organizations can take to mitigate environmental impact and navigate the challenges of our time.
