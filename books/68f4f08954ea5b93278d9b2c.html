On Time and Water

de Andri Snær Magnason

Vezi detalii
On Time and Water discusses the impact of climate change on glaciers, particularly focusing on the story of the Okjökull glacier in Iceland, which was the first to be declared dead due to melting ice. The book explores themes of time, memory, and the intertwining of human experience with the natural environment, emphasizing the profound consequences of environmental degradation.
