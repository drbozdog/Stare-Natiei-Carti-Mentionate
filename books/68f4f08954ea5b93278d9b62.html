Raise Your Game

de Alan Stein Jr.

Vezi detalii
In 'Raise Your Game', Alan Stein Jr. shares the secret principles used by high performers to enhance productivity and achieve greater success. The book emphasizes the importance of mastering the basics, cultivating discipline, and maintaining a commitment to continuous self-improvement. It offers insights gathered from over fifteen years of experience coaching elite athletes, including strategies to foster self-awareness, drive, coachability, and resilience. The book serves as a roadmap for anyone looking to elevate their performance and maximize their potential in their personal and professional lives.
