Tirania meritului

de Michael Sandel

Vezi detalii
In 'Tirania meritului' (The Tyranny of Merit), Michael Sandel explores the complexities surrounding the meritocratic system, scrutinizing how the idea of meritocracy creates division and perpetuates inequality in modern society. He argues that the meritocratic ideals often lead to a sense of hubris among the successful and impose harsh judgments on those who struggle, thereby questioning the very nature of success and its implications for the common good.
