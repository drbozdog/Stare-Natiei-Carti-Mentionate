Apeirogon

de Colum McCann

Vezi detalii
Apeirogon is a novel that explores the Israeli-Palestinian conflict through the interconnected stories of two fathers, one Israeli and one Palestinian, who have both lost daughters to violence. The narrative is structured in short sections, showcasing the complexity and depth of their experiences and the broader conflict.
