Axa Autocrațiilor

de Anne Applebaum

Vezi detalii
The book discusses the rise of autocracies around the world and the intricate web of support these regimes offer each other to maintain their power, especially in a context where democracy may be in jeopardy. It explores contemporary global politics, particularly focusing on regimes that cooperate to undermine democratic institutions globally.
