Against Meritocracy

de Jo Littler

Vezi detalii
In this book, Jo Littler argues that meritocracy is the key cultural means of legitimation for contemporary neoliberal culture. While it promises opportunity, it actually creates new forms of social division. The book is split into two parts, exploring the genealogies of meritocracy within social theory, political discourse, and working cultures.
