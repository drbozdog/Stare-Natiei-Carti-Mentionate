Oameni ultraprocesați

de Chris Van Tulleken

Vezi detalii
Dr. Chris van Tulleken's book 'Oameni ultraprocesați' explores the impact of ultra-processed foods on our health, revealing the shocking truth about how these foods, designed more for profit than nutrition, affect us. It examines the role of food additives and the industrial food system, encouraging readers to make healthier dietary choices.
