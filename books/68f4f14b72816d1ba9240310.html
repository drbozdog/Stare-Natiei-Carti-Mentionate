Principles: Life and Work

de Ray Dalio

Vezi detalii
Principles: Life and Work is a book by Ray Dalio that introduces unconventional approaches the author developed as the founder and builder of Bridgewater Associates. It offers principles that can help individuals and organizations achieve success by aligning principles with values to gain desired outcomes in both personal and professional life.
