Main Currents of Marxism: Its Origins, Growth and Dissolution

de Leszek Kołakowski

Vezi detalii
Main Currents of Marxism is a comprehensive study of the history and development of Marxist thought, encompassing its origins, philosophical roots, golden age, and breakdown through various interpretations and the ideological framework. Leszek Kołakowski offers a critical view of Marxism, detailing its impact, evolution, and the major figures involved across its history. The work was first published in Polish in 1976 in Paris, then translated into English by P.S. Falla in 1978 by Oxford University Press.
