Dumnezeul lucrurilor mărunte

de Arundhati Roy

Vezi detalii
Set in the southern part of India in 1969, the novel follows the childhood of twins Rahel and Estha who become embroiled in tragic events that irrevocably alter their lives. Through the lens of their family, consisting of a complex array of characters such as their lonely mother, a blind grandmother, and a radical uncle, the story intricately weaves themes of love, loss, and societal extremes, exploring how the smallest events can have the largest impacts.
