Palestina de la A la Z

de Mosab Abu Toha

Vezi detalii
This poem is part of the collection titled 'Things You May Find Hidden in My Ear' (2022), reflecting on the personal and collective experiences of Palestinians. It discusses themes of identity, loss, and resilience through the lens of the author's own life in Gaza.
