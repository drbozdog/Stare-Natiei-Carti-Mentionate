Miorița

de Vasile Alecsandri

Vezi detalii
Miorița is an iconic Romanian pastoral ballad, highly significant in Romanian folklore. It tells the story of three shepherds and the mystical foretelling of a murder plot by one of their sheep. The poem explores themes of fate, nature, and cultural values. The most noted version was by Vasile Alecsandri, published in 1850, although it is based on much older oral traditions.
