The Coddling of the American Mind

de Greg Lukianoff and Jonathan Haidt

Vezi detalii
The Coddling of the American Mind: How Good Intentions and Bad Ideas Are Setting Up a Generation for Failure is a 2018 book by Greg Lukianoff and Jonathan Haidt. The authors argue that overprotection is harming university students and that the use of trigger warnings and safe spaces does more harm than good. It discusses how a culture of safety and protection has emerged, inhibiting free expression and promoting a mindset that views differing opinions as threats.
