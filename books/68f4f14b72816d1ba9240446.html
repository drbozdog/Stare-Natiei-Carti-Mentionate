(My) Struggle

de Karl Ove Knausgaard

Vezi detalii
My Struggle (Norwegian: Min kamp) is a series of six autobiographical novels written by Karl Ove Knausgård and published between 2009 and 2011. The series covers Knausgård's private life and thoughts, revealing intimate details and reflections on family, education, and personal struggles. The entire series spans approximately 3,600 pages and has been well-received, generating a media frenzy upon its release.
