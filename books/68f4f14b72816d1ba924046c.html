Incerto Series

de Nassim Nicholas Taleb

Vezi detalii
The Incerto series by Nassim Nicholas Taleb consists of five books: Fooled by Randomness, The Black Swan, The Bed of Procrustes, Antifragile, and Skin in the Game. These books explore themes such as uncertainty, risk, and decision-making in complex environments, challenging conventional wisdom and proposing a new approach to understanding and thriving in a world full of unpredictability.
