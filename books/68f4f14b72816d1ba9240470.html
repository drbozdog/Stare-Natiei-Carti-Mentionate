A New York Times Bestseller • A Wall Street Journal Best Book of 2024. The CNN host and best-selling author explores revolutions—past and present—that define the polarized and unstable age in which we live. The book delves into political and economic revolutions throughout history and touches upon modern technological advances like artificial intelligence and biotechnology.
