Postwar: A History of Europe Since 1945

de Tony Judt

Vezi detalii
Postwar: A History of Europe Since 1945 is a 2005 non-fiction book by British historian Tony Judt that examines the six decades of European history from the end of World War II in Europe in 1945 to 2005. It is widely considered one of the leading accounts of contemporary European history, with particular attention to Eastern Europe.
