Vezi detalii
Scrisori către Luciliu (Epistulae Morales ad Lucilium) is a collection of 124 letters written by the Stoic philosopher Seneca to his friend Lucilius. In these letters, Seneca explores various philosophical topics, including the nature of life and death, the importance of virtue, and the acceptance of mortality as a part of the human condition.
